Welcome to the FEIRA web app

The Fish Entrainment and Impingement Risk Assessment is both quantitative and qualitative. For the quantitative portion, FEIRA uses the open source software stryke (developed by Kleinschmidt Associates) for quantifying impacts to fish populations.

Stryke is an individual-based Monte Carlo simulation model designed to assess fish entrainment and turbine blade strike risks at hydroelectric facilities. It integrates hydraulic, operational, and biological data to predict fish survival and downstream passage.

For more detailed information and documentation, visit the Stryke Project Website.
